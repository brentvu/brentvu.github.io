Slack Channels

The it3 Slack Team is made up of 5 different channels:

  • General
  • Taskmeetings
  • Wiki
  • VIT1103
  • Random

General - A place for general discussion and questions regarding it3 projects.

Taskmeetings - The channel for conducting it3 group meetings.

Wiki - All things regarding the planning and development of the it3 Wiki.

VIT1103 - A channel to discuss topics related to our Diploma of IT.

Random - This is a casual channel to share casual comments, photos and videos.
